About Us

The National Waste Management Authority is committed to ensuring sustainable, scientific, and inclusive waste management across the nation. Through innovation, regulation, and community engagement, we aim to safeguard the environment and promote a cleaner, greener future for generations to come.

Who We Are

We are a statutory body under the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, dedicated to the scientific management of solid, liquid, and hazardous waste at national, state, and municipal levels.

Our Objectives

To reduce environmental hazards by enforcing waste management policies, ensuring recycling, and promoting sustainable practices aligned with national climate goals.

Our Approach

We integrate advanced technologies with traditional practices, focusing on segregation at source, recycling, and resource recovery to minimize landfill dependency.

Community Involvement

Through awareness campaigns, partnerships with local bodies, and public participation programs, we empower citizens to become active contributors in building a cleaner India.

National Goals

Aligning with the Swachh Bharat Mission and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, we strive for a zero-waste future and sustainable urban development.

Our Mission

To establish a robust and sustainable waste management framework that ensures environmental protection, efficient resource recovery, and public health safety, while fostering innovation, accountability, and inclusivity at all levels of governance.
